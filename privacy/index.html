Short version

Naimab is designed to be local-first on desktop. Private check-ins, notes, and OS activity tracking stay on your device by default. OS activity tracking is a core feature of the desktop product that improves analysis accuracy and provides a safety cushion for the daily check-in when you miss one — you can configure tracking preferences in Settings. We collect the information needed to run the website, secure accounts, process subscriptions, and operate requested cloud features. We do not sell personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Naimab ("Naimab", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit naimab.com, join the waitlist, contact us, or use related applications, dashboards, and AI features we provide.

For the desktop product, our default model is local-first: private wellbeing entries and raw activity tracking remain on-device unless a clearly described feature requires a limited server interaction.

OS activity tracking is a core feature of the desktop product. You can configure tracking preferences — including disabling app category tracking — in Settings. We rely on this data to improve the accuracy of burnout and activity analysis and to preserve a fallback signal for the daily check-in when a user skips it. Where required by applicable law, we process this data on the basis of legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)), and you may object at any time through the app settings.

By using the service, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, do not use the service.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers. We may share information only in the situations below:

  • ·With hosting, infrastructure, analytics, email, and customer support vendors that help us operate Naimab.
  • ·With payment processors to handle subscriptions, invoices, and fraud prevention.
  • ·With AI model providers when needed to generate summaries, recommendations, or chat responses for a feature you use.
  • ·When required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the service.
  • ·As part of a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset sale, subject to appropriate confidentiality and transition protections.

We do not provide advertisers, analytics vendors, or general third parties with your raw desktop check-ins, raw notes, or raw OS activity history.

5. AI Features and Service Providers

Naimab may use third-party model providers and infrastructure vendors to deliver AI-assisted insights. When this happens, we send the minimum information reasonably needed for the relevant feature or response. For the desktop product, our default is to keep private tracking data on-device and avoid sending raw activity streams or raw private check-in history to model providers.

Examples of provider categories

Cloud hosting, database storage, transactional email, analytics, payment processing, and AI inference. The exact vendors may change over time as the product evolves.

6. Retention and Security

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain records, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and whether you maintain an active account.

For desktop-local data, retention is primarily controlled on your device. If you remove local app data or reset the desktop app, private local entries and tracking history stored there are deleted from that device.

Security measures include

  • ·Encryption in transit via HTTPS/TLS where supported.
  • ·Access controls and authentication for administrative systems.
  • ·Reasonable monitoring, logging, and abuse prevention.
  • ·Password storage using one-way hashing rather than plain text.
  • ·On supported desktop platforms, secure OS-backed storage for sensitive credentials.

No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or export certain personal information. You may also be able to unsubscribe from non-essential emails and clear cookies or local storage through your browser settings.

For desktop-local private data, you can also delete the information stored on your device directly from the app or by removing local app data on that device.

To make a privacy request, email hello@naimab.com. We may need to verify your identity before completing the request.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than the one where you live. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers, such as contractual protections and provider commitments.

9. Children and Health Disclaimer

Naimab is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Naimab is a wellbeing and productivity product, not a medical device or emergency service. Information you provide through the service may relate to stress, recovery, or mental wellbeing, but our outputs are not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Wellbeing inputs you provide — including fatigue scores, stress levels, burnout indicators, and related notes — may constitute health-adjacent or special category data under GDPR Article 9. By submitting this information through the service, you provide your explicit consent (Art. 9(2)(a)) to its processing for the purpose of delivering the burnout analysis, AI insights, and coaching features you have requested. You may withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your account and local app data, or by contacting us at hello@naimab.com.
