2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

Category Data
Account Display name, email address, hashed password
Daily check-ins Numeric ratings (1–10) for fatigue, irritability, work distance, efficiency, sleep quality, sense of autonomy, and recovery; optional free-text note
AI chat messages Messages you exchange with the AI mentor
Profile Display name, optional job title or professional context you provide
Settings & preferences Language, notification preferences, UI configuration

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

Usage & technical data

  • ·Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • ·Pages and features you visit within the app
  • ·General geographic region (country-level, derived from IP)
  • ·Session timing and error events

We do not track keystrokes, screen content, or detailed mouse coordinates. IP addresses are used only for routing and rate-limiting and are not stored long-term.

2.3 Cookies & Browser Storage

Storage Purpose Duration
Session cookie Keeps you signed in Until sign-out or expiry
localStorage Caches app settings, UI preferences, insight cache for faster load times Persists until cleared
Analytics cookies Aggregate usage analytics (if analytics is configured) — no advertising cookies Up to 1 year

You can clear cookies and localStorage at any time from your browser settings. Clearing the session cookie will sign you out.

3. How We Use Your Data

1

Burnout risk index

Computing your 0–100% burnout risk score using EWMA smoothing across your check-in history. Confidence builds after 7+ days of data.

2

Personalized AI insights

Generating recommendations, recovery plans, and CBT-based guidance via AI models. Only the context necessary for a given response is sent to AI providers.

3

Analytics & trend visualization

Displaying your historical trends, mood patterns, and burnout forecasts in the app dashboard.

4

Service operation

Authentication, billing, error tracking, and security purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 AI Providers (required for core features)

To generate insights and power the AI mentor chat, relevant parts of your check-in data and chat messages are sent to:

Provider What is sent Privacy policy
Google (Gemini API) Check-in context, chat messages (default AI provider) policies.google.com
Anthropic (Claude API) Check-in context, chat messages (optional AI provider) anthropic.com/privacy

Only the minimum context needed for the specific response is sent. We use the API in accordance with each provider's data processing terms. Neither provider is permitted to use your data to train their models under our agreement.

4.2 Payments (Stripe)

Billing for the Pro plan is handled by Stripe. When you subscribe, your payment details are entered directly into Stripe's secure form. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe may collect billing name, email, and address for payment processing. See stripe.com/privacy.

4.3 Optional Integrations

Naimab supports optional integrations you configure yourself, such as Google Calendar. These are activated only with your explicit authorization. When connected, the app may read or write data to that service on your behalf. We do not control how third-party services handle your data — please review their privacy policies.

Connecting or disconnecting integrations is always possible from your account settings.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored on encrypted cloud infrastructure (PostgreSQL database). All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

Storage Contents
Cloud database Account, check-ins, chat history, settings, billing status
Browser localStorage App preferences, session token, insight cache (stored on your device)

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the app settings. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Sensitive Nature of Your Data

Wellbeing & mental health data

Information about psychological wellbeing may be considered a special category of personal data under applicable law. We apply heightened protection:

  • ·Minimal transmission — only the specific context needed to generate a response is sent to AI providers
  • ·Encrypted storage with strict access controls
  • ·No sharing with employers, insurers, or any other parties

Naimab is not a medical device. The burnout risk percentage is a personal wellbeing signal, not a clinical diagnosis. Please consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.

8. Security

  • ·All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • ·Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • ·Passwords are stored as salted hashes — never in plain text
  • ·Access to production data is strictly limited to authorized personnel
  • ·We use Sentry for error monitoring — error reports do not contain raw user content

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to privacy@naimab.app.
