Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Your data is protected

We store your data securely in the cloud with industry-standard encryption. We never sell or share your personal information with advertisers.

1. About Us

naimab ("we", "us") is a desktop application for monitoring occupational burnout. We are committed to protecting your personal data and handling it responsibly and transparently.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

Category Data
Profile Display name, work email, job title, profession, avatar photo
Daily check-ins Numeric ratings (1–10) for fatigue, efficiency, recovery, sleep quality, and other burnout indicators; optional text comment
Weekly assessments Instrument type (BAT12/BAT23/CBI/OLBI), numeric score, risk zone
Journals Free-form weekly reflections
AI chat Messages in conversations with the AI mentor
Plans & events Tasks and calendar events (title, description, date)

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

Work activity tracking

The app passively monitors:

  • •Activity time (mouse movement, keystrokes, scrolling)
  • •Work session duration, breaks, marathons (90+ min)
  • •Activity distribution by time of day

⚠️ Important: keystrokes content and screen content are NOT recorded. Only the fact of activity is tracked, not its substance.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • 1 Burnout index calculation — via the proprietary Burnout Risk v2 algorithm (EWMA smoothing)
  • 2 Personalized insights — generating recommendations via AI models
  • 3 Knowledge graph — extracting triggers, patterns, coping strategies
  • 4 Analytics — displaying trends and forecasts in the app interface

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored securely on our cloud infrastructure, protected with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We use reputable cloud providers that comply with GDPR and other applicable data protection regulations.

Storage Contents
Cloud database Check-ins, journals, work sessions, memory graph, plans
Vector store Embeddings of scientific sources
App cache Profile, settings, session, insight cache (stored locally on your device)

6. Sensitive Nature of Data

Mental health & wellbeing data

Data about psychological wellbeing is a special category of personal data. We apply additional protections:

  • •Minimal transmission: only the context necessary to generate a response is sent to LLM providers
  • •Encrypted storage with access controls
  • •naimab is not a medical device. "Burnout Risk %" is a wellbeing signal, not a diagnosis

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
